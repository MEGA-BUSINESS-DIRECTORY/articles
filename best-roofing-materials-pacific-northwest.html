They need a roof that not only looks great but actually stands up to the weather year after year. That’s where local roofers like Homze shine as a trusted roofer company. Asphalt shingles are often the first thing people think about when discussing roof materials, and for good reason. They’re versatile, relatively affordable, and come in a wide range of colors and styles. But in the PNW, it’s not just about aesthetics—these shingles need to hold up against consistent moisture. Homze’s local crews make sure the asphalt shingles they install are high-quality, properly layered, and paired with excellent underlayment to prevent leaks. Energy Efficiency Matters

Choosing the right roofing material isn’t just about surviving storms—it also impacts your energy bills. Some shingles and metal options reflect heat better, keeping homes cooler in summer, while cedar shakes naturally provide insulation during wet, chilly months. Local roofers like Homze can guide homeowners toward roofing installation options that not only protect the house but also save money on heating and cooling over time.

Maintenance Requirements Can Vary

Every roofing material has its own maintenance checklist. Asphalt shingles might need occasional inspections after windstorms, while cedar shakes need treatment to prevent moss or mildew. Metal roofs generally require the least upkeep. They, as a reliable roofer company, help homeowners weigh long-term maintenance demands so the installation for roof matches lifestyle and willingness to care for it.

Aesthetic Appeal and Home Value

Different roofing materials can completely change a home’s curb appeal. Cedar shakes give a classic, rustic feel, while metal or tile adds a modern, sleek touch. Homze, as trusted local roofers, know which options will not only withstand Pacific Northwest weather but also enhance the home’s overall value and style, ensuring the roof is a feature homeowners love for years.

Longevity and Durability

Not all roofs are built to last the same number of years. Asphalt shingles might last 20–30 years, cedar can stretch to 40 or more with care, and metal roofs can easily surpass 50. Homze uses high-quality materials in their roofing installation to maximize durability, and they always make sure homeowners understand how each option holds up in wet winters, stormy springs, and windy days.
