By Ishfa on 14 MARCH 2024

Dr. Shilpy Dolas stands at the forefront of breast cancer treatment & breast surgeon in Pune representing a beacon of expertise, compassion, and innovation in her field. With over a decade of experience as a certified breast cancer specialist surgeon and breast diseases specialist doctor, Dr. Dolas has honed her skills through rigorous training and international fellowships. Her comprehensive approach to patient care encompasses a wide range of treatment modalities, including surgery, chemotherapy, radiotherapy, targeted therapy, hormonal therapy, and immunotherapy. Beyond her clinical practice, Dr. Shilpy Dolas is recognized for her pioneering contributions to breast surgery, particularly her innovative work in scarless surgery for breast lumps. Dr. Dolas' compassionate approach, coupled with her extensive experience and commitment to innovation, has made her a trusted advocate and ally for breast cancer patients seeking comprehensive and compassionate care in Pune and beyond.

Early Career and Training: Dr. Shilpy Dolas embarked on her journey in breast cancer treatment in Bangalore, where she underwent rigorous training at the esteemed Narayana Hrudayalaya Mazumdar Shaw Cancer Center. Her early experiences laid the foundation for her specialization in breast diseases and cancer. Specialized Fellowships: Dr. Dolas's pursuit of excellence led her to undergo a fellowship at Tata Memorial Hospital in Mumbai, a renowned institution in cancer care. This experience further honed her skills in surgical oncology, exposing her to the latest advancements in breast cancer treatment. Additionally, her international fellowship in Germany provided her with a global perspective on breast cancer, reconstruction, and aesthetic surgery, enriching her practice with diverse techniques and approaches.

Establishing Practice in Pune: Upon returning to India, Dr. Shilpy Dolas chose Pune as the base for her practice. Her clinic quickly gained prominence as a center of excellence for breast cancer treatment, reconstruction, and aesthetic surgery, attracting patients from across the country seeking her expertise.

Pioneering Techniques: Dr. Dolas's commitment to innovation is evident in her adoption of advanced surgical techniques, such as vacuum-assisted biopsy for scarless removal of breast lumps. Her groundbreaking work in this area not only demonstrates her surgical prowess but also highlights her dedication to minimizing the physical and emotional impact of breast cancer treatment on her patients.

Clinical Excellence: Dr. Shilpy Dolas's clinical acumen and expertise have earned her a reputation for excellence in breast conservation surgeries, metastatic breast cancer treatment, and management of Her-2 breast cancers. Educational Contributions: In addition to her clinical practice, Dr. Dolas is committed to sharing her knowledge and expertise with the next generation of breast cancer specialists. Community Impact: Beyond her clinical and educational endeavors, Dr. Shilpy Dolas is actively involved in raising awareness about breast cancer and promoting early detection initiatives in the community.
