Q4: What happens if the other party involved in the accident doesn’t have insurance?

It’s a common concern, and it can understandably add stress to an already difficult situation. But don't worry—our team is equipped to handle these types of challenges. If the other party is uninsured or underinsured, there are still options to explore. We can pursue a claim through your own insurance policy, such as using an uninsured motorist clause if you have one. Alternatively, if the at-fault driver is untraceable (in cases of a hit-and-run), we can work with the Motor Insurers’ Bureau (MIB) to ensure you’re still compensated. We’ll explore every avenue to make sure you’re not left without support.
