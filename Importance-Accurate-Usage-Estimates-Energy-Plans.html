The Pitfalls of Traditional Estimation Methods

Historically, energy providers often asked consumers to estimate their usage within broad ranges. Unfortunately, these estimations were prone to inaccuracy due to their reliance on rough approximations rather than precise data. Consequently, customers would often find themselves facing unexpected surges in their bills or stuck in plans that didn't align with their actual consumption habits.

The Impact on Consumer Choice

Accurate usage estimation is the linchpin in the process of selecting the most suitable electricity plan. Consumers armed with precise usage information can make informed decisions, choosing plans that match their consumption patterns and lifestyle. However, inaccurate estimations can lead to a mismatch between the plan and actual usage, resulting in either overpaying for unused energy or facing high fees for exceeding allowances.
